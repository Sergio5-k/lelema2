Who We Are

Lelema Consultants, founded in 2015 in Sandton, South Africa, is a trailblazer in data analytics and business intelligence. We empower organizations worldwide to unlock the full potential of their data, driving smarter decisions and sustainable growth.

Our team of data scientists, business analysts, and industry veterans brings advanced degrees and decades of experience across finance, retail, manufacturing, and healthcare. We deliver bespoke solutions that turn complex data into actionable insights, optimizing operations and enhancing customer experiences.

With a relentless focus on innovation and client success, we partner closely with our clients to deliver measurable results. At Lelema, data isn’t just numbers—it’s the key to transforming your business.
